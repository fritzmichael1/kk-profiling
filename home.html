About Us

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of Gaban is dedicated to empowering the youth in our community. We aim to encourage active participation in local governance, foster youth development programs, and create a supportive environment where young people can thrive.

The SK works closely with the community, government organizations, and youth groups to implement programs that address the needs and aspirations of the youth. Through various initiatives, we provide opportunities for education, employment, and community service.
